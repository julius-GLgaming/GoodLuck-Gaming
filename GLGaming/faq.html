Good Luck Gaming FAQ


Last Published: February 2017

1. How are winners chosen?
We will use the random drawing services from random.org. You will be able to audit the process on their website if you have any concerns.

2. How do I know if I won?
Each raffle has an approximate date that we will announce the winner (typically one week after the raffle deadline). We will contact you based on your preferred method of contact (usually email) and let you know that you won!

3. When will I receive my prize?
Digital prizes will be emailed to you approximately one week after the sweepstakes deadline. Physical prizes will be mailed to you approximately one week after the sweepstakes deadline.

4. What if I didn’t receive my prize?
If you do not receive your digital or physical prize, please contact our Chief Customer Happiness Officer: anish@gl-gaming.com

5. What if I love Good Luck Gaming and have some ways I can help you?
We are always looking for celebrity streamers and amazing charities to support, so if you would like to get in contact with us, please contact Chief Outreach Officer: sampo@gl-gaming.com
