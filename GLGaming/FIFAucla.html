Playing in support of UCLA C4C

UCLA Anderson’s Challenge for Charity (C4C) is the largest charitable organization at the Anderson School of Management. Last year, Anderson C4C raised $42,000 and volunteered more than 5,000 hours for its three select charities: Special Olympics, Junior Achievement and Project ECHO. UCLA Anderson is one of nine West Coast business schools in the MBA Challenge for Charity organization that competes annually for the coveted Golden Briefcase. The competition is comprised of fundraising, volunteerism and the annual Sports Weekend hosted at Stanford University each April. We strive to help develop Anderson MBAs with a lifelong commitment to community involvement and social responsibility. Challenge for Charity has a 501(c)(3) nonprofit standing.
