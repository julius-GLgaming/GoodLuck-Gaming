Good Luck Gaming Official Rules


Last published: February 2017

No purchase is necessary in order to enter events or use gl-gaming.com. Entry is subject to all local laws. Void where otherwise prohibited or restricted by law. All federal, state, and local laws and regulations apply.
The below official rules describe the structure and process for entering, participating, and conducting events as recognized by Good Luck Gaming. Users may enter any event by the alternative methods of entry and are not required to make payment.

1. Structure of Events:
Good Luck Gaming (“Sponsor”) partners with charities, companies, and individuals to source and provide exclusive prizes, including the grand prize for each event. These Official Rules apply to all events unless there are specific exceptions for an event. In these cases, the exceptions will be written on the event page.

2. Entering an Event:
There are several methods for customers to gain entry into an event, which include an alternative method of entry: Visit gl-gaming.com, search for the event in which the customer wants to enter, pick an entry package, checkout and complete payment before the end date of the event. Each event page will specify exactly the number of entries a customer receives and the prizes which the customer is eligible to win. Visit gl-gaming.com, search for the event in which the customer wants to enter, scroll to the bottom of the page, click on “Alternative Method of Entry,” complete the entry form before the end date of the event. Legibly hand write the name of the event, your name, home address (no PO Boxes), phone number, age, email address (optional), and the statement “I have read and agree to Good Luck Gaming Official Rules.” Postmark this document to by the date specified on the event page. Alternative mode of entry customers will receive an entry into the sweepstakes.

3. Entry Limit:
The maximum number of entries a customer can be awarded is limited to 1,000,000 entries person per event.

4. Eligibility:
Customers must be at least 18 years old to enter and legal residents of the United States. All entrants must submit truthful verification information including but not limited to name, age, and location. If an entrant does not meet eligibility rules, he or she will be removed from the sweepstakes and may be liable for legal penalties. Employees of Good Luck Gaming, Inc. are not eligible to participate in sweepstakes. Entry is subject to all local laws. Void where otherwise prohibited or restricted by law. All federal, state, and local laws and regulations apply.

5. Event Time Period
Each event starts at 12:00:01 AM Pacific Standard Time and ends at 11:59:59 PM Pacific Standard Time on the days specified on the individual event page. If there are exceptions to the event time period, it will be specifically stated on the individual event page.

6. Event Winner Selection
Approximately one week after the close of the event, Good Luck Gaming will choose a potential winner using the website random.org. Good Luck Gaming will notify the potential winner via the preferred communication method in the individual’s account. Once a potential winner has been chosen, the individual must provide confirmation to Good Luck Gaming’s eligibility and sign appropriate paperwork in order to be confirmed as a winner. If necessary, Good Luck Gaming can request a background check on the winner. Good Luck Gaming is not required to refund the winner if he or she does not meet eligibility criteria. If the potential winner does not meet event eligibility requirements, Good Luck Gaming will continue to work through the list of potential winners as generated by random.org until a true winner has accepted the prize.

7. Marketing Waiver
By accepting a prize, the winner grants permission to Good Luck Gaming to use his or her name, photograph, voice, and videos of the event for promotions and marketing by the company or prize providers without receiving further compensation.

8. Privacy and Data Collection
Good Luck Gaming reserves the right to collect personally identifiable information in order verify the eligibility and identity of customers and potential winners. Sensitive data (including and not limited to credit card, home address, date of birth) entered by a customer in order to complete registration for an event will not be disclosed to outside entities that are not associated with the particular event in which the customer enters.

9. Limitation of Liability
Prizes can not be transferred or substituted, except that Good Luck Gaming can substitute a Prize of equal or greater value. If a “Winner” is unable to use the Prize for any reason, then he or she forfeits the right to the Prize or any other substitute of lesser, equal, or greater, value. Winners who are unable to use their Prize will instead receive a credit to their “Good Luck Gaming” account for the amount of Entries spent on that particular Event.

10. Waiver and Indemnification
By entering an event the customer agrees to be bound by the official rules and waive rights to bring any claim, action, proceeding against Good Luck Gaming or any of the entities responsible for providing prizes (as described above) which are in connection with the event the customer entered.

11. Winner List
For a winner list, visit GLG Prize Winners. The winner list will be posted after winner confirmation is complete.
